About my supposed father, Michael

My supposed father, Michael Morka, is 50–70 years old. He is a religious leader with the church, Celestial Church of Christ(CCC). CCC is a spiritual church that operates similar to fetish or juju. The difference is insistence that it’s practices must be godly and avoidance of colors such as black and red to indicate it is not affiliated with fetish or juju practices.

Michael brought a lawyer, Barr.Kalikwu, a relative of the person claimed to be dead during the April 19, 2022 attack at my gated premises without my asking, to Asaba police command on April 21, 2022.

I was transferred to Asaba police command from Agbor police command on April 20, 2022. The lawyer Barr.Kalikwu listened to my account and collaborated with police to falsify a statement claiming an accidental discharge occurred without my consent. Police officer blessing wrote the statement herself, she did not write the detailed account of events stating that I am a licensed hunter and faced multiple robberies and assassination attempts which were reported to the police. Michael is aware of my account of events from Agbor police command to Asaba police command. He claims he went to the hospital at Agbor and initially informed me that the intruder at my property, Happy Uboh(claimed to be dead), was being taken to a traditional doctor(local doctors that treat with roots and herbs). He later claimed that Happy Uboh was sent to FMC(Federal medical centre) Asaba, a hospital. I successfully argued that I did not commit a crime but Barr.Kalikwu who happens to be related to the persons accusing me falsified a statement to allow police charge me with a capital crime of murder. It would have been impossible to charge me with a crime if the police investigated and receive my witness accounts. The negative actions of Michael gives reason to suspect Michael intentionally brought Barr.Kalikwu, a relative of the persons claimed to be dead, Happy Uboh, to sabotage and imprison me with falsification of an accidental discharge when there is no evidence to show anyone was shot during the attack at my premises.

Refusal to inform the accusers of the facts.

Michael refused to inform the accusers of the facts when he engaged them during the 35+ days of spent at Asaba police command.

Michael insisted that he cannot inform them I was innocent because the only thing he was required to do was beg the accusers.

I argued against this logic as it was important to inform anyone accusing me of a crime that I did not commit a crime. Why would I beg without informing the accusers of my innocence?. I refused to provide further monies for resolution of the matter until Barr.Aliu (my lawyer) mediated and brought peace between me and Michael at Asaba police command. Michael agreed he would follow the plan to inform the accusers of the facts while I was making efforts to ensure police admits the facts that I was a robbery and assassination attempts compared to an accidental discharge as falsified by Barr.Kalikwu and police.

Complaint to Michael about robberies and repeat attacks

I complained to Michael about the robberies and repeat attacks before April 19, 2022, his response was that I cannot fight a community or a group of armed robbers. I argued that one man who is right can fight a group of wrongdoers.

Michael misappropriated the ₦600,000 and other monies without a result or response. He feigns ignorance when I enquire about monies i allocated for my defense and release. I requested that he engage the accusers with the facts of what occurred, he elected to engage chiefs and other persons, spend money without accountability while refusing to inform these persons that I was innocent as I stated, unaware of the accusation, and a victim of the attack on April 19, 2022, at my gated premises.

Michael attempted to imprison me by obstructing justice. He claims to be protecting the people that attacked me because they were persons from the community.

I’ve asked Michael if I should be accused of murder when I’m innocent and the actual attackers allowed to accuse me and roam freely while I’m detained.
